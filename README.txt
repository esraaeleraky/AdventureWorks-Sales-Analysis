📅 Time & Product Deep Dive Analysis
<img src="Dashboards/Time & Product Deep Dive.png" width="700"/>
🔍 Insights
In 2006, a significant drop in profit was observed in June.
A deeper analysis was conducted by examining the Average Price and Average Discount for that month.

It was found that one product had a notably high price while also receiving a high discount.
This combination likely contributed to the decline in profit.

💰 Territory Profitability
<img src="Dashboards/Territory Profitability.png" width="700"/>
🔍 Insights
Some regions generate high sales but maintain low profit margins.

Discounts are not the primary cause.

➡ Bikes generate high revenue but deliver lower margins.
➡ Accessories generate higher margins but contribute less to overall revenue.

💡 Final Understanding

Bikes act as the primary revenue driver.
Accessories act as the primary margin driver.

Overall profitability depends on the product mix.
